To add your professional photo to the portfolio:

1. SAVE YOUR PHOTO:
   - Save your professional photo as "profile-photo.jpg"
   - Place it in the same folder as index.html
   - Make sure the file is named exactly: "profile-photo.jpg"

2. PHOTO SPECIFICATIONS:
   - Recommended size: 300x300 pixels or larger
   - Format: JPG, JPEG, or PNG
   - File name: "profile-photo.jpg"

3. WHERE IT WILL APPEAR:
   - Hero section (main profile picture)
   - About section (secondary image)

4. ALTERNATIVE NAMES:
   If you want to use a different filename, update these lines in index.html:
   - Line with: src="profile-photo.jpg"
   - Change to your actual filename

5. TEST:
   - Open index.html in your browser
   - Your photo should appear in both sections

Your professional photo will automatically be displayed in the portfolio once you add the file!
